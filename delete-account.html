Account Deletion Policy
⚠️
Permanent Action
Once your account is deleted, it cannot be recovered. All your data will be permanently and irreversibly erased.
⚙️ DATA PRIVACY
Delete Your Account
We respect your right to control your personal data. You can delete your CivilConnect account using either of the two methods below.
📱
Option 1 — Delete Instantly In App ⚡
The fastest way. Open the app and navigate to:
👤 Profile → 🗑️ Delete My Account
Your account and all data will be deleted immediately after two confirmations.
📧
Option 2 — Request via Email
Processed within 7 business days
1
Email us at civilconnect.vot@gmail.com
2
Use subject line: "Delete My Account"
3
Include the email address registered with your CivilConnect account
4
We will verify your identity and process your deletion within 7 business days

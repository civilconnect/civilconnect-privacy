Account Deletion Request

Request Account Deletion

We respect your right to control your personal data. If you'd like to delete your CivilConnect account and all associated data, please follow the steps below.

How to Request Deletion
  1. Send an email to support@civilconnect.app (update this address)
  2. Use the subject line: "Delete My Account"
  3. Include the email address registered with your CivilConnect account
  4. We will verify your identity and process your request within 7 business days
⚠️ Please note: Once deleted, your account cannot be recovered. This action is permanent and irreversible.
What Data Gets Deleted
  • Your profile information (name, email, photo)
  • All posts, comments, and activity history
  • Notifications and preferences
  • Any other personally identifiable information
Data Retention

Some data may be retained for up to 30 days in encrypted backups before being permanently purged. Data required by law (e.g., transaction records) may be retained for the legally mandated period.
